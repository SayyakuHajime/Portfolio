Based in Bandung, West Java. Studying Machine Learning with a focus on natural language processing and information retrieval. Known online as @SayyakuHajime. I build things to understand them, and write to figure out what I actually think.

Background

I came to machine learning through a combination of curiosity about how language works and frustration with how poorly existing tools handled Indonesian text. That frustration turned into a project, which turned into a research interest.

My current focus is on representation learning and how neural networks build internal models of language — both what they capture and what they systematically miss. I'm particularly interested in the gap between benchmark performance and real-world robustness.

Outside of ML, I think a lot about systems thinking, the philosophy of knowledge, and what it means to understand something versus being able to predict it.

How I work
