01 · Identity

The brand aesthetic is Editorial Dark Academic: warm near-blacks, amber gold accents, and a serif display typeface that carries the weight of ideas. Every visual decision should feel intentional — like a well-typeset journal, not a tech landing page.

The tone is precise but not cold. Curious but not performatively enthusiastic. It respects the reader's intelligence and time.

07 · Usage Guidelines
✓ Do
Use serif display type for all major headings and titles. Let the typography breathe — prefer generous line heights and spacing. Use gold accents sparingly, for the most important elements only. Pair monospace with serif for contrast.
✗ Don't
Don't use purple gradients, Inter/Roboto, or any "tech startup" palette. Don't clutter with icons or badges. Don't use accent gold as a background color. Don't mix more than 3 font weights on a single page.
