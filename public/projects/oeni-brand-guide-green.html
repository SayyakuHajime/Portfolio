05 — Voice & Guidelines
Brand Rules
Do
  • Lead with Forest or Moss on dark backgrounds — they anchor the green identity
  • Use the full green gradient (Forest → Moss → Fern → Sage → Mist) for depth
  • Pair Cormorant SC headers with Jost body for schedules and pricing
  • Use Pinyon Script as a single accent line — never for body copy
  • Earth and Parchment add warmth; use them to break up all-green compositions
  • Give the symbol generous clear space — 1× its own height on all sides minimum
  • Use botanical line-art details (leaves, stems) as supporting texture only
Don't
  • Never use neon or lime greens — stay within the muted, earthy green family
  • Don't use Sage text on Mist backgrounds — contrast is too low
  • Avoid stretching, recolouring, or rotating the logo mark
  • Don't place the logo on heavily patterned or photographic backgrounds without buffer
  • Never use Pinyon Script for pricing, navigation, or any text longer than 8 words
  • Avoid generic wellness photography — favour warm natural-light, minimal editing
  • Don't centre-align body copy blocks longer than 3 lines
